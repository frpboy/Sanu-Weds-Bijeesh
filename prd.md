# Product Requirements Document: Project Eternal Scroll

## 1. Vision and Goal
To create a "Cinematic" wedding tribute for Sanu and Bijeesh that transcends traditional website design. The project, titled **"Project Eternal Scroll"**, aims to create an immersive, 3D celestial journey that celebrates their union and family-led journey.

## 2. The Experience: 4 Major Pillar Changes
1.  **From "Static Space" to "The Star Tunnel"**: A dynamic 3D starfield that moves with the user's scroll, creating a "Warp Speed" effect.
2.  **From "Vertical Info" to "Horizontal Storytelling"**: A horizontal "Film Strip" journey using GSAP ScrollTrigger to present their story as a cinematic timeline.
3.  **The "Interactive Nebula" (The Climax)**: A transition into a swirling 3D Nebula at the end, which explodes into gold heart particles upon user interaction.
4.  **The "Gate" & Audio Sync**: A blurred 3D entry point that requires user interaction to start the cinematic soundtrack and dive into the experience.

## 3. Key Features & Components
*   **CelestialEngine (3D Background)**: A persistent R3F background with stars and wireframe icosahedrons linked to scroll position.
*   **IntroGate (Audio & Entry)**: A full-screen overlay to manage audio context and transition into the site.
*   **StorySection (Horizontal Timeline)**: A 500vw wide horizontal scroll section with glassmorphism memory cards and parallax effects.
*   **Tribute (Final Wish)**: A concluding section with a personal wish and interactive "Send Blessings" button (confetti and WhatsApp redirect).

## 4. Background and Context
*   **Arranged Marriage**: A family-decided union, focused on the coming together of two families.
